Game Rules

Yu-Gi-Oh is a trading card game where players duel each other using decks of cards. The objective is to reduce your opponent's life points to zero.

Each player starts with 8000 life points. Players take turns drawing cards, playing monsters, spells, and traps, and attacking their opponent's monsters or directly.

The game has various phases: Draw Phase, Standby Phase, Main Phase 1, Battle Phase, Main Phase 2, and End Phase. Each phase has specific actions that can be performed.

Players can win by reducing their opponent's life points to zero, causing them to draw from an empty deck, or achieving specific win conditions set by certain cards.

For more detailed rules, visit the official Yu-Gi-Oh website or refer to the rulebook included with your cards.
